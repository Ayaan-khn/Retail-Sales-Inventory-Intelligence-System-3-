# Business Insights Summary

## Executive Snapshot

- Total net sales: 7.69M.
- Gross sales before discounts: 8.58M.
- Discount amount: 889.87K.
- Total orders: 1,615.
- Total customers: 1,445.
- Average order value: 4,761.06.
- Units sold: 7,078.
- Late shipments: 458.
- Low-stock inventory records: 190.
- Inventory value: 19.82M.

## Sales Performance

The business generated 7.69M in net sales from 1,615 orders. Average order value is strong at 4,761.06, suggesting that the business sells relatively high-value products or bundled purchases.

## Brand Performance

Trek is the leading brand with 4.60M in sales, significantly ahead of Electra and Surly. This indicates that Trek should receive strong attention in inventory planning, promotions, and category-level reporting.

## Category Performance

Mountain Bikes is the top category with 2.72M in sales. Road Bikes and Cruisers Bicycles are also major contributors. These categories should be monitored closely for stock availability and seasonal demand.

## Store Performance

Baldwin Bikes is the highest-performing store with 5.22M in sales. It substantially outperforms Santa Cruz Bikes and Rowlett Bikes, making it an important benchmark for store-level performance practices.

## Staff Performance

Marcelene Boyer and Venita Daniel are the top contributors by sales and order volume. Their performance can be used as a benchmark for sales productivity and customer handling.

## Inventory Risk

There are 190 low-stock store-product records. Low stock should be prioritized for high-selling brands and categories to avoid revenue loss from stockouts.

## Shipment Performance

There are 458 delayed completed orders. This indicates an operational improvement opportunity in fulfillment planning, delivery coordination, or required-date commitments.

## Recommended Business Actions

- Prioritize replenishment for low-stock products in high-sales categories.
- Review the shipment process for stores or products with repeated delays.
- Use top-performing staff as benchmarks for training and sales process improvement.
- Maintain strong inventory depth for Trek and Mountain Bikes.
- Compare Baldwin Bikes operations against lower-performing stores to identify best practices.
